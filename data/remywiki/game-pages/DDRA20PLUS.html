DanceDanceRevolution A20 PLUS

Release Information

  • Release dates:
    • 20th anniversary model cabinets: July 1st, 2020
    • Other cabinets (excluding Europe): July 6th, 2020
    • Europe: January 14th, 2022
  • Slogan: "想いをPLUSして、集えACEたちよ" (Omoi wo PLUS shite, tsudoe ACE-tachi yo; PLUS your feelings and gather the ACEs)

General Information / Changes

  • Updated release of DanceDanceRevolution A20, with new background animations and a new set of BGM.
    • This makes A20 PLUS the first PLUS release in the arcade DanceDanceRevolution series since DanceDanceRevolution 4thMIX PLUS.
    • Plus signs have been added to background videos on the song select and score overview screens.
  • First North American DanceDanceRevolution version released via online update.
  • A new "level measurement" feature, entitled "LET'S CHECK YOUR LEVEL!" is now available; if the player fails the first stage, they will then play an alternative chart of This Beat Is....., after which they will be given a recommended difficulty level and songs to play. If they are playing with Premium Play, they will then proceed to the second stage as normal.
    • This is only available when playing without an e-amusement pass, or if the player has not cleared a chart rated Level 7 or higher.
    • This feature was also accessed in VERSUS PLAY if any of the players failed a song, regardless if the other player cleared it or not. This was fixed on July 16th, 2020.
    • A new LIFE GAUGE type, "FULL CLEAR" has been added. It is depicted as a completely filled, brighter version of the NORMAL life gauge, slowly pulsing white. Misses and NG's do not deplete the gauge, allowing the player to pass a song without any danger of failing.
    • This gauge only shows up when playing "LET'S CHECK YOUR LEVEL!".
    • "LET'S CHECK YOUR LEVEL!" is not included on the European version of the game.
  • The crowd audio clips are much quieter and play less often during gameplay.
  • All of the licenses exclusive to the 20th anniversary model cabinets of A20 remain exclusive.
  • At launch, all of the songs region locked in the North American release of A20 were still region locked in A20 PLUS.
    • On February 5th, 2021, the region locked songs from DanceDanceRevolution A were made available.
  • European machines do not have e-amusement support due to regulations by the European Union. However, this version does not require an e-amusement connection.
    • EXTRA STAGE, all EXTRA STAGE-related unlock systems, DDR SELECTION, CHALLENGE-only songs, and gameplay options requiring e-amusement or Premium Play are unavailable in the European version because of this.
  • The European release of A20 PLUS is based on the February 2021 version of the game for upgraded machines, the December 2021 version of the game for machines sold from 2022 to 2023, and the final February 2022 version for machines sold since 2024. The song list differs as follows:
    • Licensed songs removed in-game on the May 21st, 2021 update of DanceDanceRevolution A, and in future versions of DanceDanceRevolution A20 PLUS, remain absent.
    • Upgraded machines retain eight songs from the POPULAR SONGS folder in DanceDanceRevolution A: Believe, Break Free, Happy, Hillbilly Shoes, I Want You To Know, Shut Up and Dance, Time Of Our Lives and Wake Me Up. New machines do not include these songs.
    • On new machines, the 11 GOLDEN LEAGUE songs from DanceDanceRevolution A20 are unlocked, and can be played without an e-amusement pass. On upgraded machines, songs released after the 7th GOLDEN LEAGUE (Starlight in the Snow, Glitch Angel, Golden Arrow and CyberConnect) remain absent.
    • On new machines sold since 2024, most licensed songs that are not in the POPULAR SONGS folder are removed. However, the four default GOLDEN LEAGUE PLUS songs (DIGITALIZER, Draw the Savage, MUTEKI BUFFALO and Going Hypersonic) are unlocked, and can be played without an e-amusement pass.
  • European machines come with a brand new marquee for A20 PLUS.
    • Machines upgraded from DanceDanceRevolution A still use that game's marquee.
  • New songs: 124 (Asia), 94 (North America)
  • Total songs: 1,003 (Asia), 965 (North America)

EXTRA STAGE

The Result Star system introduced in DanceDanceRevolution X3 vs 2ndMIX is used in A20 PLUS, and is only available on Premium Play. When nine Result Stars are obtained, and FINAL STAGE is cleared, EXTRA STAGE will be played. Both players' stars are combined, and progress carries over to the next round if EXTRA STAGE was not reached. Each player can earn a maximum of three stars per song. During EXTRA STAGE, the LIFE GAUGE modifier is restricted to LIFE4.

Star(s) Requirement
★★★ For attaining the grade AAA.
★★ For attaining the grade AA-, AA, or AA+.
★ For attaining any other grade except E.
★ For a Full Combo. (Cumulative)
★ For playing with LIFE4 or RISKY modifier. (Cumulative)

Unlockable Songs

EXTRA SAVIOR PLUS

The EXTRA SAVIOR song folder from DanceDanceRevolution A returned on July 16th, 2020, now called EXTRA SAVIOR PLUS. These songs, when unlocked during EXTRA STAGE, can thereafter be played during any stage on Premium Play.

In A20 PLUS, EXTRA SAVIOR works more akin to EXTRA ATTACK from the 2014 game, where charts must be unlocked in order: BEGINNER/BASIC, DIFFICULT, then EXPERT. Song unlocks are based on total points earned on a play of an unlockable song. Each chart has a different point requirement to unlock it:

  • BEGINNER is unlocked on the first play regardless
  • Single/Double BASIC requires 500,000 points
  • Single/Double DIFFICULT requires 1,000,000 points
  • Single/Double EXPERT requires 2,000,000 points

As a bonus, 100,000 extra points are given automatically on each play of a song. Using the modifiers CUT1/CUT2, Jumps OFF, or Freeze OFF will lower clear progress as they result in less score earned.

Any songs can be unlocked in any order with the sole exception of TYPHØN. which requires all nine OVER 200 other EXTRA SAVIOR songs to be fully unlocked.

Single and Double charts must be unlocked separately.

DANCERUSH STARDOM × DanceDanceRevolution SUMMER DANCE CAMP 2020

The DANCERUSH STARDOM × DanceDanceRevolution SUMMER DANCE CAMP 2020 event is a collaboration event between DanceDanceRevolution A20 PLUS and DANCERUSH STARDOM. From July 7th, 2020 to September 7th, 2020, stamps could be earned by playing either DanceDanceRevolution or DANCERUSH STARDOM. Three stamps are offered once per day, one for the game being played and two for the opposite game. By collecting a certain amount of stamps for a specific game, songs will be unlocked for the respective game.

These songs can also be unlocked through EXTRA SAVIOR PLUS since March 8th, 2021.

GOLDEN LEAGUE PLUS

GOLDEN LEAGUE PLUS started on July 30th, 2020, for 20th anniversary model cabinets only. Please see the DDR A20 page for more information.

Rewards are given at the end of each Period. The latest GOLDEN LEAGUE song will first appear in GOLD CLASS, before it will be relegated to SILVER CLASS after two periods, followed by BRONZE CLASS after four periods, before it finally becomes unlocked by default four periods later.
