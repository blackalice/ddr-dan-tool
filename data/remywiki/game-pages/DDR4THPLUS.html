DanceDanceRevolution 4thMIX PLUS

Release Information

  • Release Date: December 28th, 2000

General Information / Changes

  • Second PLUS version in the series.
    • Its Solo release, DanceDanceRevolution 4thMIX PLUS Solo, marked the final Solo cabinet release.
  • An "All Music" folder has been added, allowing players to choose any song without restrictions, unlike in the original DanceDanceRevolution 4thMIX. However, character selection is chosen by roulette.
  • Several songs have received new MANIAC charts, but their old charts are still kept on the fourth selection on difficulty menu as "MANIAC-S" for Single or "MANIAC-D" for Double.
    • In later DanceDanceRevolution releases, these new charts were re-added as CHALLENGE charts, with the original ones being used as their EXPERT charts.
  • NINZABURO was renamed FURUHATA'S THEME, marking the first time in the DanceDanceRevolution series that a song has been renamed.
  • The songs in the NONSTOP course OFFICIAL 1 were changed in 4thMIX PLUS.
  • Last DanceDanceRevolution game to have NONSTOP courses until DDRMAX2 -DanceDanceRevolution 7thMIX-.
  • Last DanceDanceRevolution game with 6-Panel and BATTLE charts.
  • All hidden songs from DanceDanceRevolution 4thMIX have been unlocked for normal play.
  • The color labeling on the music select screen has been expanded to showcase new songs:
    • Yellow for brand new songs introduced in 4thMIX PLUS.
    • Green for songs introduced in 4thMIX or 3rdMIX PLUS.
    • Blue for all other old songs.
  • Total songs: 150
    • At 150 songs, including nearly every song from past non-CLUB VERSiON arcade versions alongside 14 new songs, DanceDanceRevolution 4thMIX PLUS had the most songs of any DanceDanceRevolution release at the time of its release.
