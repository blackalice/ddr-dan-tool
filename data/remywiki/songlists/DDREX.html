The CS songs are listed in their own folder in DanceDanceRevolution EXTREME. For the sake of consistency with the other games' songlists, they are listed in their arcade game debut. Same with BEMANI crossovers.

DanceDanceRevolution Solo originals have been moved to a new Solo folder, since the Solo series is listed separately from the main series' songlists.
